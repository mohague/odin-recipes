Lasagna


Description

Lasagna, a classic comfort food, is a baked dish featuring layers of wide pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This recipe simplifies the process, making it perfect for beginners. The hearty meat sauce, infused with aromatic herbs, complements the smooth béchamel, creating a symphony of flavors. Assembled in layers and baked until golden, this lasagna is a satisfying and crowd-pleasing meal.

Ingredients
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500g ground beef (or pork)
  • 1 onion
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, Italian herbs
Steps
  1. Make the meat sauce: Sauté onion and garlic, add the meat, then the tomatoes. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Make the bechamel: Melt butter, add flour, then slowly add milk while stirring.
  3. Assemble: In a baking dish, alternate layers of pasta, meat sauce, bechamel, and cheese.
  4. Bake: 30 minutes at 180°C (350°F) until golden on top
