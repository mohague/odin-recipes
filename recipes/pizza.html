Pizza


Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish known for its crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and endless topping possibilities. This easy homemade pizza recipe is perfect for beginners. With just a few basic ingredients, you can create a delicious pizza that’s fun to make and even more fun to eat!

Ingredients
  • 1 ready pizza dough
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • 1 Onion
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Add the sauce: Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  3. Add cheese and toppings: Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top, then add your favorite toppings.
  4. Season: Drizzle with olive oil, and add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Bake: Place the pizza in the oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
