Soup


Description

Soup is a warm and comforting dish, ideal for chilly days or when you need something light and nourishing. This easy vegetable soup combines fresh veggies, herbs, and a flavorful broth, making it both healthy and delicious. It’s quick to prepare and can be enjoyed as a starter or a main meal.

Ingredients
  • 2 carrots
  • Potato
  • 1 Onion
  • Fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
Steps
  1. Prepare the vegetables: Peel and chop the carrots, potatoes, onion, zucchini, and celery into small pieces.
  2. Sauté the onion: In a large pot, heat the olive oil and cook the chopped onion for a few minutes until soft.
  3. Add the vegetables: Add the carrots, potatoes, zucchini, and celery to the pot. Stir and cook for about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in the broth: Add the vegetable broth, then bring everything to a boil.
  5. Simmer: Reduce the heat and let the soup simmer for about 20 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
